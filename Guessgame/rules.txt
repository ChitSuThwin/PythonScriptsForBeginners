Hello everyone Welcome to

"Guess the word" - game

HOW TO PLAY :

The game begins with the COMPUTER Choosing a word. Later the User ,ie 'you' ; have to guess the word 

you can type charactor by charactor 
ie :
 Guess a word : 'a' 
 Guess a word : 'b' 

 you can also guess the entire word itself:

 Guess a word : 'like this' 
 Guess a word : 'this_is_a_word' 
 Guess a word : 'apple' 

 anything in between will be considered as wrong

You got 12 life in total , that means you can only guess 12 times.

If you fail to do so , then you lose
